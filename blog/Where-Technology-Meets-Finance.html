Where Technology Meets Finance
Read Article
Barış Sürücü
25 April 2019
4 MIN READ



Every paradigm shift in the market has its burst impact on financial side of the market. The 21st century paradigm shift of data driven decisions had its impact on financial markets too. In fact, it changed all discourse in the current aspect; thus, named as fintech (financial technologies). The overall impact obviously cannot be unseen; however, the effects on the grassroot levels need closer inspection.

There are two main discourse in this topic; one is related with present and the other one is related with future; otherwise named as, trapping in today and the obsession of future, respectively. The former billion-dollar companies that are erased from the history of business and tiny startups that are became the future-setters even from today. Those two faces of the financial markets are not specific to said markets; but they are the major driving forces of the enterprises that are found and currently active.

The latest paradigm shift caused a huge breakthrough in the market that changed the scope of impact of the even tiny changes in technology. That is, the financial markets are evolved in such a way that their driving forces being affected by even infinitesimal changes in technology. However, the changes in technology means a lot than it sounds or being heard. The huge improvements have an exponential impact as well as a creative destruction effect in the market. Some of those improvements may look like they are being forgotten; but, destructive effect of the market forces enlarges their area and timing of impact.

Say hello to Big Data

Data is the oil of 21st century. Now it is the most valuable resource for industries from different sectors. As data gets bigger (be it due to globalization or rise of supranational companies), the tools to collect, interpret, and process data becomes complex. Thus, word complex is a good analogy of needs-technological-innovation in 21st century. The agents in the financial sector, generates huge amounts of data every day from customer insight to banking behaviors. Hence, big data’s generation itself is an immense innovation of technology in fintech!

Blockchain but Cooler

I don’t know how many times the world blockchain is written in bold in internet in last 5 years. Nevertheless, it is something to think about in every aspect of it. Blockchain, distributed ledger system that puts bitcoin to its core, is in last 5 years showed its true potential in startups that decentralized business models can be sustained more efficiently. Thus, although not restricted to financial systems, blockchain is making its way up to universal real sector; hence, becoming mainstream in next few years.

New Instinct is AI with Learning and Teaching Capacities

No technology essay is done without referring to artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and internet of things (IoT). This is one of them. Not predict but imagine a concept that uses big data and needs tremendous amount of data processing units. However, the system is used by a human and humans make mistakes (we all know that). Now imagine, a couple of individuals must use dozens of said system to process big data, you cannot imagine the amount of error in this place. This is where AI, ML, and IoT come in. The above example is a typical every day life of a financial market agent. However, artificial intelligence units are used to reduce cost and save time in finance sector as well as ML and IoT to enhance customer experience while adding value to final services/product. Thus, an individual’ process power quadruples with AI and ML while IoT enhances the impact area.

Technology Driven Decisions

This one is clear. New implementations to the market always result in higher than predicted outcomes. The newly implemented mobile banking systems what predicted was ease of use in banking systems and financial actions. However, last few years proved that mobile banking almost entirely changed the way consumer thinks, behaves and of course spend. The Fintech Firms popped-out almost immediately in global scale to offers more choice and freedom to consumers. All digital platforms, innovative application and so on was a work of financial systems. Let me correct the last sentence “was a work of technology where it met finance”.
