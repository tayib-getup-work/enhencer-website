Advantages of Instant Notifications
Read Article
Serkan Oğuz
23 May 2019
3 MIN READ



In the age of interactive marketing, consumer behavior decides everything. What interactive marketing means changes it core sense as some part of interaction comes from both supplier and producer parties. However, giving full incline over a product provides a glimpse to producer that other party is interested over the product; thus, they would need constant updates on current situation. Hence, instant notifications are vital in this sense as SaaS operations like Enhencer must alert the important messages to user when needed.

Previous article in our blog stressed the benefits of having a mobile app in hand. Thus, in order to get full insight in real time users must reach platforms immediately. This may only happen with mobile apps. In fact, especially SaaS operations has an essential need for mobile access as their nature suggest high mobility and versatility. Hence, access to real time statistics, conditions, and alternative projections is vital as decision taking highly time sensitive; that is, speed of action is important. Therefore, having mobile app in hand relives your position as you can easily reach vital data and take decision accordingly. However, there is something more to add these benefits: push notifications.

Push notifications are the relatively younger when compared to email notifications and such. Nonetheless, their capabilities to reach more real user make them unique and superior. Real user here means multiple things but the best way to put it is that a real user is the one that gave consent for the upcoming news about the product. Thus, unlike email notifications, push notifications are sent to user that are directly interested on the content. Moreover, the mobile device nature makes the push notification more like real-time updater where instant news is sent, and a feedback is sought.

Therefore, user is reported on the important subject such as a change in variable, a fault in any part or an analytical discrepancy due to any problem. Thus, acting immediately becomes an informal job. Enhencer provides a subtle notification system for user experience is a game changer in the delicate situation where instant decisions are vital. Therefore, as Enhencer understands the benefits of notifications systems, implementation of them help the both sides. The benefits can be listed as:

  • Subscription is easy related to email notification subscription system. Thus, both parties may benefit from that as user engages in minimal amount while producer gains more conversion
  • When connected to internet, if mobile device is in hand the messages by the software or the producer itself delivered instantly; thus, helping quick acting in every situation.
  • Last but not the least, notifications ease the users’ experience with the product; thus, immediate updates and event notifications can be followed via notification system to track possible changes in variable.
