Real Time Computing and Mobile App Notifications
Read Article
M. Ahmed Tayib
12 July 2019
4 MIN READ



When someone says real time computing, the main objective must be on the real time part not on the computing part. No matter how important it is to have a huge computational power, the instant analyzing power is totally different; thus, it outruns the physical capacity of any computer. On the other hand, the fact that mobile devices are in everywhere and they are more powerful than the Apollo 13 rocket, is another issue (or more of an improvement) that needs to be addressed. Thus, real time computing and mobile app notifications must be examined for better understanding of 21st century paradigm of management.

Real time computing is like live output mechanism where outputs are constantly updated as an input is changed. If the change in the input has a huge impact on the output, one can differentiate the variance without a program. However, the infinitesimal changes in the inputs that, when built up, have an impact on the output can only be observed by the specific computers. In the time series analysis, not every impact seems as random, however, there is always a randomness in the situation. Hence, the randomness can be explained by some theories for calculations. The pure randomness, randomness with trend, and randomness with drift and trend.

When it comes to calculations, the infinitesimal changes must not be overlooked as they can be a small but influential part of a trend or a drift. Thus, having a real time computing capacity helps a lot to comprehend all changes in an instant manner. Getting those instant computations is important because the change in a trend or a downward trend in the data can be overlooked without a careful eye as in parts, they show nothing but in full picture we can see a dramatic change in the data set. Therefore, the in order to intervene the manner without being too late, the data analysis must be done in real time and kept updated constantly.

The main object of this post is mobile app notifications, as we had talked in out previous posts, the mobile devices are taken over the world by entering almost every pocket. Rather than just talking and testing or surfing in the internet the devices are now real powerhouses that are more complicated than the moon landing devices of 70’s. therefore, in order to had full use of those devices in the management field specifically, the operations are running from web based software or with companion apps that are increasing the benefits of real time computing. Although, real time computing and live output terms are used interchangeably, the mobile app notifications are the main difference in their practice. That is, the real time computing is about updated inputs and outputs which can be delivered via mobile app notification services; however, the live output mechanism is more like having constant flow of outputs from the program. More specifically, the real time computing is artificial intelligence that uses new variable and updates the outcome, while live output mechanism is update in the program due to operator’s intervention. Thus, mobile app notifications are vital in the former as involvement must be after than the output update in real time computing rather than before as like in live output.

To sum up, as statistical theory suggested, there is always a randomness in the situation; hence, the intervention must be made before it too late to involve in situation with an unexpected turn of events such as trend change or a drift shock. Therefore, having instant notifications helps mangers to keep an eye on data before something happens.
