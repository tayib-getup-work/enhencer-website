7 Benefits of Having a Mobile App in Hand
Read Article
Ümit Selahattin Öner
21 May 2019
4 MIN READ



Last few decades were the era of paradigm shifts, multiple sectors and areas experienced a noticeable change of solutions and related problems. Thus, observing enough movement in the market showed that mobile apps are not only branding exercises for enterprises but also, they are tacit lifestyles of end-consumer. That is, once optional mobile existence is now a must for enterprises from all level as the consumer behavior changed its direction to mobile devices from web just as it happened when TV commercials hit the market and newspaper sales got plumped.

That paradigm shift has multiple folds as every change becomes linked to everyday life. However, lifestyle change of consumers put everything forward. The cloud computing made possible almost all data is shared across multiple devices, Internet of Things opened the doors for so called smart wearables as well as smart solutions; thus, mobile devices became the part of everyday life from unlimited aspects. Finally, with the help of Artificial Intelligence mechanics and ever-improving capacities of mobile devices, the capabilities of mobile apps sky-rocketed. The quick response, real-time notifications, and mobility across devices encouraged users to want more while already giving more.

User experience is now the core of every enterprise strategy as there are unlimited number of firms doing pretty much same thing. In fact, the little nuances from every one of them matters as the user-experience enhanced with little tweaks to end-products. That’s why supplier needs to source distinctions from others as well as itself in a constant manner. That is, continual updates are now must for endorsing the product of the enterprise. The need of mobile apps arose from there. As consumers take their mobile devices close to themselves and as mobile devices’ capacities enlarges with almost every innovation, the application paradigm would be shifting constantly to create new problems while presenting new solutions to them.

Therefore, the benefits of having mobile apps are both-sided where one side is consumer whose life gets easy, and other side is enterprise which creates a product differentiation to collect market demand. However, the said benefits can be collected under single roof to understand them perfectly. In fact, as mentioned above enhancing user experience via tweaks benefits the enterprise more than it does to consumer. Thus, from the perspective of producer; but, for the enhanced experience of the consumer, having mobile app in hand has 7 benefits.

  • Mobile devices usage is increasing rapidly; hence, producer can reach more people with a simple mobile app that does the job.
  • Mobile apps enhance product’s and producer’s visibility in terms of branding as reaching what is supplied becomes super easy.
  • As people carry their mobile devices almost always, channeling new information and providing notices about the news becomes easy.
  • Mobile apps save the consumer from the hassle of web sites as experience is simplified; thus, information can be reached easily. Also, mobile apps working speed is more than web sites so both sides benefit from faster connection.
  • Mobile apps are good places to offer support services; thus, they can enhance your stand with the customers.
  • Mobile apps can be used as a tool for marketing in general so that influence range can be expanded.
  • The best part of the mobile apps is that they can provide live notifications on matters related to customer’s experience on topics such that analytical outcomes and/or I/O faults. For more information on live notifications.
