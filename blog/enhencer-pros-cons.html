What are the cons and pros of Enhencer?
Read Article
M Ahmed Tayib
27 February 2019
5.0 Minutes to Read



Enhencer is SaaS platform focuses on the business intelligence and data visualization concepts after the emergence and popularity of the machine learning methods and launched in METU Technopolis in last quarter of 2017. Our customers are located in Turkey and the US. Even though we have just launched the platform, company capital is made by our sales and only a tiny amount of funds and investment. So company growth enables our team to add the new features and customize the platform according to the desires of our customer. Every platform is not for everyone and all kind of things in life comes with cons and pros, here is Enhencer’s Cons and Pros. We also recommend other platforms to our customers in case we are not the best options for them.

Pros

  1. Vast data types: One of the advantages of Enhencer is the opportunity to describe the variables/columns with different data types such as Likert, EPS(Enhencer Promoter Score), numeric, categorical and ID to get the most benefit from the analyse since each data types offers a different analyse screens while our team is constantly reviewing and adding the new data types according to the needs of our customers.
  2. A person without a data-science background can run the decision-tree algorithm and reduce the complexity of the data with a click on Enhence. The results are easy to interpret since the outputs are displayed in a pseudo-form such as the most profitable segment and increase the sales or which paths cause the loses, dropouts, fraud, churns etc. However, there are further options to display and configuration settings for a technical user who looks for more detail.
  3. Our developer teams had dedicated to designing a platform to make data analysis and modern tools accessible to people who are from all across the backgrounds and set the principles to improve user experience and engage with the users in the most efficient way. The outcome is a distinguished intuitive user interface with a high-quality design. So one of the advantages is that any person can acquire a full comprehension of the platform with a 20-minute walkthrough.
  4. Customer support is one of the distinguishing properties of Enhencer platform. While we’re launching online communities to strengthen the power and knowledge of Enhencer’s user community, we constantly support users to prepare/clean their data and make decisions about the target choices and discuss the reports and train the users on how to use Enhencer’s platform.
  5. Enhencer team has a high consideration of security principles. We kept in mind that the corporate businesses might desire to secure their data while enabling their employees to analyse. Once you imported the data, analysis screen comes and you can perform all kind of analysis and review the visualizations and also display the raws for a particular result and even export it but there isn’t access the whole data imported to analyse.
  6. Subscription prices vary across the data storages and the active users of the company. We beware of the extreme prices in the data analytics platforms and we offer a suitable monthly subscription fee and you only buy the platform if you decide it benefits your company after demo-account.

Cons

  1. While importing a csv, txt or xlsx(Excel) files, connecting to a database or the survey platforms like Typeform are only a matter of a click, the platform doesn’t perform the database tasks such as join. However, our support team guides you to prepare your data to deploy to Enhencer and convert the data files into the appropriate formats.
  2. The platform doesn’t have a mobile application integration, yet. Neither the cloud platform app.enhencer.com has a responsive interface for the mobile users.
  3. You can save and display reports only on the Enhencer cloud directory though you can export the visualizations. The reports are not editable in terms of a text editor and don’t provide an automated presentation option.
  4. If you have a data and looking for a tool to analyse it through modern tools which provides you with the insights, make predictions about the future outcomes and save the reports then Enhencer is a good fit for your company without a priori knowledge requirements. Our prices are best for the companies where 5-14 users will be actively using the platform. Our customers work in the Online-Game Companies, CPG, Automotive, Finance, Insurance, Retail and Merchandise industries and also we work with the government bodies and the public services such as UNICEF projects in juvenile assessment.

If we are a good fit for your company/organization then you can see more information on our website enhencer.com. Hope this message helps. You can also send me a direct message to ask for anything further.

Otherwise, I would recommend you to search for other BI tools which might be a better fit for your company. There are various articles online which guides you to review BI software and explain what to keep in mind before buying a software.
