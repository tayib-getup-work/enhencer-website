Effects

Effects is a powerful tool that shows the variables that effects the target variable the most. This means there could be hundreds of variables in the data but among them these are the ones to affect the target variable the most.




  1. Effects can be accessed by simply clicking the "Effects" button.
  2. The right side of the screen shows important variables for the model. The variables are sorted from“Most Effective” to the “Least Effective”.

Interpretation:
Enhencer with the help of machine learning algorithms found out that;

  • Out of 24 parameters in the data fed to the model 12 of them have effect on customer churn probability. This means these are the variables that contribute in finding which customers will churn while all the other variables do not contribute so much.
  • The most effective parameter on customer churn is Offline Usage (hours per week).

If you click any of the variables from the right panel in Effects tab then you will be shown the group by Effect of the Target variable and the selected effective variable. The Group By is explained here.
