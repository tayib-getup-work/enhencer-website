Startup of the Year: Enhencer

  • 06 May 2019
  • Ümit Selehattin Öner


The final event of the entrepreneurial ecosystem TechSquare, organized by KoçSistem, was held at UNIQ Maslak.

KoçSistem is the largest IT integrator in Turkey, and TechSquare is KoçSistem’s acceleration platform for B2B initiatives focusing on the Internet of Things, Business Solutions, Analytics and Security.

A jury consisting of C-level executives and directors in startup ecosystem in Turkey has chosen Enhencer as "Startup of the Year" and awarded 100.000 Turkish Liras as 1st prize, after evaluating 12 startups who made it to the finals, on metrics such as vision, market potential, and revenue model.



Enhencer offers the most widely used and most effective Machine Learning algorithms to all business units in a fast, easy-to-understand, visualized interface. It enables all data-savvy business professionals to utilize ML algorithms for modelling and prediction.

Shortly after a year since it’s inception, Enhencer is utilized by many enterprises internationally, and in near future the team is planning to reach different users from every level to help them apply ML modelling and predictions to their business processes.

We would like to thank everyone supporting us in this journey.
