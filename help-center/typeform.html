Typeform

To connect and import data from Typeform first select Typeform from the design screen.




Choosing Typeform will redirect to typeform login screen.




Provide your Typeform credentials and login to your typeform account.




After successfully logging into the account you will be asked to grant access permission to Enhencer by clicking “Accept”. Accepting the terms from Typeform will redirect you to the following page.




To import the desired data choose the workspace and the Typeform project that you wish to import the data from and hit “ok”.
