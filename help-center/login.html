Login Screen

If you are connecting Enhencer for the first time or your saved login credentials are reset for any reason, you will be directed to the login page through your browser.




The suggested browser to be used to access Enhencer is Google Chrome.

  1. Enter your e-mail (used to register to Enhencer) and password and clickLogin to Enhencer button.
  2. If you have forgotten your passport click Forgot Password? link or consult your Enhencerlink or consult your Enhencer Admin.
  3. If you do not have an account, you can click Sign up. In some local installations, Sign up may be disabled.
  4. In the cloud, you can also sign up to Enhencer using your LinkedIn or Google account.

For cloud-based Enhencer head over to the official website enhencer.com and sign in from there or simply click the following link Sign in. For the local server please contact you it department for the link.
