Segments

"Segments" is a useful section that shows segments of the target variable and their features. After the model is created, segments can be accessed simply by clicking the Segments button.




  1. The right side of the analyze screen, in this case, shows all the segments available for the target variable.
  2. The area labeled as 2 represents the features of the selected segment.
  3. The number in the right edge represents the sample size of the selected segment. Segment size shows how many individual records fall into that segment.
  4. The number in the left corner represents the Lift for the segment. Lift is the total impact of the segment or the node, compared to the entire data average.

Segments are sorted from the highest lift to the lowest lift.
