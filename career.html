Full-Stack Developer

We are looking for an experienced full-stack developer to help us build a brand new exciting analytical platform which is accessible, responsive, robust and user-friendly. This is a great opportunity to join a rapidly expanding machine learning R&D company working with both private and government institutions in Turkey and abroad.

Required Skills
  • Having a B.S. degree from computer or software engineering departments (M.Sc. is a plus)
  • Minimum 1 year of experience in web development
  • Minimum 1 year of experience in data preparation on SQL based system.
  • Good knowledge of AngularJS, Node.js, MongoDB, Html5, CSS3, and Bootstrap is a must
  • Good knowledge of JQuery, and React.js is a plus
  • Understanding of modern software development principles, design patterns and architectures
  • Interested in big data and machine learning solutions
  • Male candidates should have no military obligations or deferred at least two years
Opportunities
  • Working with a small, dedicated and dynamic team
  • Flexible and relaxed working environment
  • Good hardware options to work with (2 external monitors etc.)
  • Facilities provided by METU TECHNOPOLIS
About Enhencer
Enhencer is a cloud-based Self-service Machine Learning platform. We are on a journey to bring Auto ML to the hands of millions, and we are looking for teammates to help us accomplish this goal. We would predict human behaviors and complicated cases through speed, volume, and diversity provided by big data structures. The analytical tools that we develop help us to create more precise and compact solutions for both user-friendly and impartial reports. The processes from the preparation of big data up to enhancement of decision support models are supported by the real-time operating software products which our R&D team has developed.

Our team is a comprehensive mixture of experienced, dynamic and interdisciplinary experts on machine learning and big data analytics.
